## What is Computer Science?

Computer Science is the scientific and practical approach to computation and computational systems. It studies algorithm design, data structures, programming languages, software engineering, and computer systems.

## Key Areas

1. **Programming & Software Development**
2. **Data Structures & Algorithms**
3. **Computer Systems & Architecture**
4. **Database Systems**
5. **Computer Networks**
6. **Artificial Intelligence**

## Basic Concepts

- **Algorithm**: A step-by-step procedure for solving a problem
- **Data Structure**: A way of organizing and storing data
- **Programming Language**: A formal language for instructing computers
