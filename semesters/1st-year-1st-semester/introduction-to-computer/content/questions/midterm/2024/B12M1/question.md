**Exam Type:** Midterm

## Instructions

- Answer all questions
- Use clear and concise language
- Draw diagrams where necessary

## Questions

### Question 1 (20 marks)

Define Computer Science and explain its major areas of study.

### Question 2 (25 marks)

a) What is an algorithm? Explain with example.  
b) Differentiate between algorithm and program.

### Question 3 (25 marks)

Explain the following terms with examples:

- Data Structure
- Programming Language
- Operating System

### Question 4 (30 marks)

a) Describe the evolution of computers from first generation to current generation.  
b) What are the key components of a computer system?
